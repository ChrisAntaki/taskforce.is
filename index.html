Our Mission

We are a volunteer task force dedicated to making tools. Specifically, tools to allow people to organize and defend the internet.

You might not have heard of us.
It's because we work in the background to build an organizing infrastructure so citizens can amplify their voice and be heard.
Also, we are pretty new!

We coordinate closely with groups like EFF, Fight for the Future, Free Press, Mozilla and others to defend the internet.
